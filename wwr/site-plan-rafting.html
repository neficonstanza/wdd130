Overview

Purpose

The website will share information about Dry Oar Rafting co. with customers who love nature and rivers. Through sharing detailed information, we will be able to generate more revenue by attracting more customers. We will help clients choose their next trip in a convenient and enjoyable way using our White water Rafting page. The page will include the prices of tourist guides and information that they may need to make the best decision. In addition, the page will include an updated list of policies and rules that the client must follow in order to enjoy a pleasant visit and trip. Here at Dry Oar we take rafting to a new level where you will have "An unforgettable experience".

Audience

Target Audience

  • Adventure seekers
  • Single Adults (Ages 21 to 45)
  • Family oriented (Mid 11 to 50)
  • Lovers of water activities
  • Trill seekers
  • Nature Lovers
